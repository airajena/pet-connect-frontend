Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the adoption process take?

Typically 3-7 days after application review and approval.

Can I visit the animals before adopting?

Yes! We encourage meet-and-greets by appointment.

What documents do I need for adoption?

Valid ID, proof of residence, and adoption application form.

Do you provide post-adoption support?

Yes, we offer ongoing support and guidance for new pet parents.
